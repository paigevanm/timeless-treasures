Timeless Treasures

My name is Danna. My husband, our two dogs, and I live in a 100 year old home that used to be my great-grandparents. Our home served as the insipration for Timeless Treasures. A few years ago, I began to collect flowers and greenery from our yard to make FAMILY resin blocks for our family members. From there, my resin projects have expanded and the possibilities of what I create are endless.

Reach out through call or text. I'd love to preserve your precious memories in a Timeless Treasure.
